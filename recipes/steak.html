Two pieces of a brilliant medium rare steak

Savor the exquisite taste of a perfectly seared medium rare steak, a dish celebrated for its balance of tenderness, juiciness, and robust beef flavor. Cooked to perfection, this steak features a deeply caramelized crust with a warm, reddish-pink center, making it a favorite among steak lovers and fine-dining enthusiasts.

Ingriedients

  • Steak – (Ribeye, Sirloin, Filet Mignon, or New York Strip) - 1-inch thick
  • Salt – (Preferably coarse sea salt or kosher salt)
  • Black Pepper – (Freshly ground for best flavor)
  • Butter – (Unsalted, for basting)
  • Garlic – (2-3 cloves, smashed or minced)
  • Fresh Herbs – (Thyme, rosemary, or parsley for aromatics)
  • Olive Oil – (Or another high smoke point oil like avocado oil)
